FOLDABLE SMARTPHONES

THE FUTURE OF MOBILE PHONES

It’s no surprise that foldable smartphones have become very popular. Foldable smartphones are one of the most innovative and exciting trends in the mobile industry, offering a unique combination of portability and versatility, allowing users to switch between a compact phone and a tablet in a single form factor. Here are the advantages and challenges of using foldable smartphones and a comparison of the top three foldable smartphones of 2023.

What are foldable smartphones and why are they important?

Foldable smartphones have a flexible screen that can bend or fold in half, creating two different form factors: a phone and a tablet. Since the first commercial launch of a foldable smartphone in 2018 by Roloye Flexpai 1 , Samsung has innovated the technology with the Galaxy Fold over the years, and other brands like Google and OnePlus have joined the trend. Foldable smartphones are important because they represent a new paradigm in mobile design and user experience. They offer several benefits over conventional smartphones, such as:

  • Larger screen size: Foldable smartphones can provide a bigger and more immersive display for watching videos, playing games, reading, and multitasking. For example, the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 5 has a 7.6-inch main screen when unfolded, which is comparable to a small tablet.
  • More productivity: Foldable smartphones can enable more efficient and flexible work scenarios, such as running multiple apps side by side, using a stylus for note-taking or drawing, and connecting to external monitors or keyboards. For example, the OnePlus Open has a dedicated multitasking mode that lets you split the screen into three windows and drag and drop content across them.
  • More creativity: Foldable smartphones can unleash more possibilities for content creation and consumption, such as taking stunning photos and videos, editing them on the go, and sharing them with ease. For example, the Google Pixel Fold has a 5x optical zoom lens that can capture amazing details and powerful photo editing software that can enhance your images with AI.

However, foldable smartphones also face some challenges and limitations, such as:

  • Higher cost: Foldable smartphones are more expensive than conventional smartphones, due to the complex engineering and materials involved in making them. For example, the Samsung Galaxy Z Fold 5 costs $1,799, which is almost twice the price of Samsung Galaxy S23.
  • Lower durability: Foldable smartphones are more prone to damage and wear and tear, due to the moving parts and the delicate nature of the flexible screen. For example, they have a plastic inner screen that can develop creases and scratches over time.
  • Lower compatibility: Foldable smartphones are not fully supported by all apps and services, due to the varying screen sizes and aspect ratios. For example, some apps may not scale properly or display black bars on the foldable screen.

What are the top three foldable smartphones of 2023?

Based on my research, I have selected the following three models as the top foldable smartphones of 2023:

OnePlus Open 2

The OnePlus Open has superior multitasking software to other foldable devices. It has a 6.31-inch Super Fluid AMOLED outer display shielded by a ceramic guard and a 7.82-inch Flexi-Fluid AMOLED inner display shielded by a very thin glass 2. Both are 2K resolution display with 10-bit Color and 120HZ LTPO 3 adaptive refresh rate 2.

The OnePlus Open runs a Snapdragon 8Gen2 processor, which is the most powerful chipset on Android phones. It has a triple-camera setup system that includes a 48MP main camera with OIS, a 64MP 3X telephoto lens with OIS, and a 48MP ultra-wide with no OIS 2. It has a 4805 mAh battery with 67W wired charging. It starts at $1699 in the USA.

Samsung Galaxy Fold 5 3

Samsung Fold is said to be the go-to device when considering a foldable phone because Samsung has innovated and set the benchmark for foldable phones over the years.

The Galaxy Fold 5 is the fifth foldable from Samsung and has improved excellently as it improves upon previous versions with its excellent cameras, tons of multitasking options, performance, and gorgeous display. It has a 6.2-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X outer display shielded by Gorilla Victus 2 and a 7.6-inch Dynamic AMOLED 2X inner display shielded by a very thin glass. Both displays have a 120HZ LTPO 3 adaptive refresh rate 3.

The Galaxy Fold 5 is equipped with a triple camera setup system that consists of a 50MP main camera sensor, a 12MP Ultra Wide, and a 10MP 3X telephoto with 30X space Zoom 3. The main display has a 4MP under-display camera and they all have a flaw detection feature (Intelligently detects flaws, so it's nearly impossible to take a bad shot.) 3.

The Galaxy Fold 5 runs on Snapdragon 8Gen2 and a 4400mAh battery with 25W wired charging. It is rated IPx8 for water resistance and starts at $1800 in the USA 3.

Google Pixel Fold 4

Google Pixel Fold, the AI powerhouse of foldable smartphones is Google’s first attempt at a foldable phone. it has some impressive features and some drawbacks, drawbacks such as the noticeable large bezels and a weaker chipset.

However, the Google pixel fold has some impressive build quality. Here, let me tell you about it.

It has a triple rear camera setup, which consists of a 48 MP Quad PD with OIS, a 10.8 MP ultrawide camera, and a 10.8 MP dual PD telephoto camera with 5X optical zoom and up to 20X Super Rez Zoom 4. It has a 5.8-inch outer display protected by Gorilla Glass Victus and a 7.6-inch inner display protected by Ultra Thin Glass with protective plastic layers. Both displays are OLEDs with full 24-bit for 16 million colors 4.

It runs on Google Tensor G2 chip, which is not as powerful as Qualcomm Snapdragon, but it runs at a decent speed.

The Pixel Fold starts at $1799 in the USA and has VPN by Google at no extra cost 4.

Conclusion

Foldable smartphones are the future of mobile devices, as they offer a new way of interacting with technology and enhancing our daily lives.
